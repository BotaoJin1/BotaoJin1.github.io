About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at the University of California, Santa Barbara, under the supervision of Professor Ruimeng Hu. My research focuses on stochastic analysis, backward stochastic differential equations (BSDEs), and their applications in stochastic control and machine learning.

Research Interests

  • Backward Stochastic Differential Equations (BSDEs)
  • Stochastic Control and Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman PDEs
  • Machine Learning Methods for Stochastic Systems
