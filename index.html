About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics and Applied Probability (Financial Mathematics Emphasis) at the University of California, Santa Barbara, under the supervision of Prof. Ruimeng Hu. Before joining UCSB, I obtained my Master of Science degree in Computational and Applied Mathematics from the University of Chicago, where I worked with Prof. Daniel Sanz-Alonso. My current research focuses on Rational Inattention with its interaction with adversial games.
